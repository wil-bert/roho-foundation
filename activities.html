Activities

Here is a list of awesome activities that we get to engage in with the children:

  • Giving them a chance to express themselves for example through dancing.
  • Arranging story telling sessions where everyone is encouraged to speak their mind in order to explore their imaginative side more.
  • Organizing fun shows such as teaching them how to make origamis.
  • We also organise sports day where each person chooses which game to play or teams to support.
  • One that comes naturally, is that we have fun.Lots of it, so we'd be happy if you joined us.
