Activities

Embracing Talents

We encourage all our children to embrace and improve on their respective talents. Erica Jong once said “Everyone has talent. What's rare is the courage to follow it to the dark places where it leads.”

Group bonding

This Activity teaches our young students to work together, learn to work as a team and archieve different tasks with the help of each other.

Community Outreach

With our community outreach, we encourage the children to reach out to the other less fortunate in the socirty and also lend them a helping hand.

Mental Health

Every once a week, we bring in a specialist to help with the children. Talk to them and get a chance for a one on one with a child, through this we get to understand a child's mental health and kknow how to help.
